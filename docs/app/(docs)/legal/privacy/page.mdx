# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 1, 2025**

OSN Developers Team ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use SudoBot, a Discord Moderation Bot. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information provided by users of SudoBot.

## 1. Information Collected

**a. Discord User Data:** SudoBot may collect and process user data from Discord servers where it is installed, which are: user IDs, usernames, and profile pictures. This data is used to provide the bot's functionality within the Discord platform.

**b. Command Data:** When users interact with SudoBot by using commands, the content of those commands may be processed by the bot. This includes but is not limited to text, images, and other media shared through the bot's features. Along with that, it may make use of various Discord resources such as roles, channels etc to give users the functionalities they want. It may store role IDs, channel IDs, member IDs for processing and ensuring commands work.

**c. Automatic Moderation Data:** SudoBot can perform automatic moderation if enabled by server administrators. In that case, it may also collect channel IDs, role IDs, message IDs, member IDs so that the automatic features work.

## 2. Use of Information

**a. Bot Functionality:** Collected data is used to provide the intended functionality of SudoBot within Discord servers. This includes moderating content, executing commands, and assisting with server management.

**b. Improvements and Analytics:** We may use aggregated and anonymized data for analytical purposes to improve SudoBot's features and performance. No personally identifiable information is used for these purposes.

## 3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of user data. However, no data transmission over the internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Users acknowledge and accept this inherent risk when using SudoBot.

## 4. Third-Party Services

SudoBot may utilize third-party services to enhance its functionality. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle user data.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide the services offered by SudoBot or as required by applicable laws. Users have the right to request the deletion of their data by contacting us at rakinar2@onesoftnet.eu.org.

## 6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update and modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of SudoBot after changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of those changes.

## 7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rakinar2@onesoftnet.eu.org.

By using SudoBot, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.
